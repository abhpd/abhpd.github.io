HOW IT WORKS?

MANN KI BAAT is a simple pocket supplement which is user friendly, not looking for the complex mind drives.

Getting Started.

DOWNLOAD our application MANN KI BAAT from play store for a soft accessing or visit our website.

Need to Enroll.

Getting signed up is easy for MANN KI BAAT and we help you get along the way.
You just need to have a small formal login by providing name, age and sex. You DON’T need to worry, as the login is automatically done if the details are not entered, making it friendly with the users.

Need some Assessment.

You are taken to the question page, where we ask you some psychiatric questions in the user friendly format.
Your answer to the questions are recorded and processed and Mann Ki Baat detects whether you are depressed or stressed.

WHAT’s NEXT?

If Mann Ki Baat finds you stressed, it directs you to the chat bot. It is stranger to stranger chat bot where user can chat with anyone anonymously.
If Mann Ki Baat finds it as depression, it takes you to our IDT(Image Description test), where your response to the images are recorded by webcam. Then the report is displayed to the user, letting him know whether he/she is depressed or not.

Services with Mann Ki Baat.
